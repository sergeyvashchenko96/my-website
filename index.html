Alpha Cars Herts.

With over 25 years of experience in managing licensed Private Hire companies in different regions, Alpha Cars Herts was established in 2019 licensed by Hertsmere council and is based in Potters Bar covering the whole region of Hertsmere and beyond.

We specialise in both business and private customer sectors and offer all services of transporting passengers and parcels all over the country.

All our drivers are licensed, DBS checked, smart, polite, professional and knowledgeable.

We are open 24/7 with a range of late model cars which include 7 & 8 passenger vehicles.

We undertake all types of journeys and with working from home currently the ‘new norm’ serving local people, businesses, schools, hospitals and airports, moving people, packages and documents all over the district and all over the country, we are here to facilitate that this is carried out in a smooth and stress free manner.

We are proud to offer our friendly and professional services to the local area where Alpha Cars Herts is the ‘car service that cares’ and places their customers first.
